PAYMENT QR CODE SETUP
=====================

Place your UPI payment QR code image here as:
payment-qr.jpg

How to get your UPI QR code:
1. Open Google Pay / PhonePe / Paytm
2. Go to your profile or business settings
3. Find "QR Code" or "Payment QR"
4. Download or screenshot the QR code
5. Save it as "payment-qr.jpg" in this folder (public/)

The QR code will be displayed on the payment page when customers click "Buy Now"

File requirements:
- Filename: payment-qr.jpg (or payment-qr.jpeg)
- Format: JPG/JPEG
- Recommended size: 500x500 pixels or larger
- Must be a valid UPI QR code

After adding the file, the payment flow will work:
Products → Buy Now → Payment Page (shows QR) → Customer scans & pays
